Joel Edgerton

Mini Biography

Joel Edgerton was born on June 23, 1974 in Blacktown, New South Wales.He went to Hills Grammar School in the Western Suburbs of Sydney, and after leaving, he attended Nepean Drama School in 1994. Joel has done many projects on stage and off, but most people will recognize him from his work on the Australian television series The Secret Life of Us.

Awards: 1.AACTA Award for Best Supporting Actor The Great Gatsby 2014 2.AACTA Award for Best Lead Actor in a Television Drama The Secret Life of Us 2002
