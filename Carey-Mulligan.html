Carey Mulligan

Mini Biography

Carey Mulligan is a British actress, born May 28, 1985, in Westminster, London, England.Her first major appearance was playing Kitty Bennet in Pride & Prejudice.

Awards: 1.BAFTA Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role An Education 2010 2.National Board of Review Award for Best Actress An Education 2009 3.London Critics Circle Film Award for Actress of the Year An Education 2010
